Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, sauce, and meat.

It is warm, cheesy, and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Cook the meat sauce
  3. Layer ingredients
  4. Bake in oven
