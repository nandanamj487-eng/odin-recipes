Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish made with layers of cheese, sauce,veggies and meat.

It is warm, cheesy, and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Activate yeast
  2. Make and knead the dough and let it rise
  3. Make sauce
  4. Shape the pizza
  5. Add toppings and Bake in oven
