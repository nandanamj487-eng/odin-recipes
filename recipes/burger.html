Burger

Description

Burger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked meat patty (traditionally beef) placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll

It is warm, cheesy, healthy and kids fav.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Patty
  • Ground beef/veggies

Steps

  1. Shape: Form ground meat or your veggie mix into round, flat patties.
  2. Season: Sprinkle both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook: Grill or pan-fry the patties until they get a nice brown crust.
  4. Melt: Place a cheese slice on top during the last minute of cooking
  5. Toast: Slice your buns in half and toast them lightly in a pan.
  6. Build: Put sauce on the bun, add the patty, and top with veggies.
